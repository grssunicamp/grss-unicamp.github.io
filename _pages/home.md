## Geosciences and Remote Sensing at UNICAMP

The **IEEE GRSS UNICAMP Student Chapter** is an institutional student organization affiliated with the  
**IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Society (GRSS)** and based at the **University of Campinas (UNICAMP)**.

Founded in **2010**, the chapter promotes academic integration, technical training, and scientific outreach focused on Earth observation technologies.

### Main areas of interest

- Remote sensing  
- Geoprocessing  
- Earth observation and geospatial analysis
