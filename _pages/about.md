## Who we are

The **IEEE GRSS UNICAMP Student Chapter** was founded in **2010** and is sponsored by the  
**IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Society (GRSS)**.

The chapter is composed of undergraduate and graduate students from **geology**, **geography** and related areas at the **University of Campinas (UNICAMP)**.

## Our mission

Our mission is to:

- Promote education and training in remote sensing and geoprocessing  
- Encourage scientific and technical development  
- Foster collaboration between students, researchers and professionals  
- Disseminate geospatial technologies and Earth observation applications  

Through seminars, workshops and academic events, the chapter connects students with the international GRSS community.
