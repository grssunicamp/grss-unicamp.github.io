<div class="grss-section">

The **IEEE GRSS UNICAMP Student Chapter** was founded in **2010** and is sponsored by the
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Society.

The chapter promotes academic integration, technical training and outreach activities
related to **remote sensing**, **geoprocessing** and **Earth observation**.

Based at the **University of Campinas (UNICAMP)**, the chapter connects students with
the international GRSS community.
