About Us

The IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Society (GRSS) UNICAMP Student Chapter is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting education, research and scientific exchange in geoscience, remote sensing and Earth observation.

Our activities include technical seminars, workshops, outreach initiatives and collaborative events connecting students, researchers and professionals.
